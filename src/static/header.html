Instructions to Record

**Be a Dubber**

- Use the latest 'Mozilla Firefox' browser for an error free dubbing experience.

**Choose a Video**

Using add video option you can add a new Youtube video and start dubbing or you can discover videos by browsing through differnt categories in the gallery or you can find specific videos by using our search function.

**Start Dubbing**

On the Dubbing studio page, Click on the Enable microphone button below the video and share your Microphone with the website. Then Click RECORD and Start dubbing, let your imagination take over.

**Features of the Dubbing studio page**

- You can view the original video before recording, by just clicking on it.

- If you wish to start dubbing the video from a certain time (Ex. From 30th sec or 150th sec), insert the time in seconds in "Start Video Time in Seconds" option. By default the video starts from 0 secs.

- You can Pause, Resume and Stop the recording.

- Once your dubbing is over, Use the PREVIEW button to watch your dubbing.

- (Optional) Use the "Adjust Video Start Time" option to adjust the sync of the audio with the video.

- Enter details and click FINISH to post the dub.
